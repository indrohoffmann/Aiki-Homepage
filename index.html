27.7 Aiki&Band is performing live in Kirkkonummi’s Wohls Gard summer restaurant. First time it’s opportunity to hear Aiki’s new song „Teisiti ei saakski“. There will be more Aiki’s own songs plus some own cool arrangements from songs like „Daddy cool“ „Moonlight shadow“ „Wannabe“ from Spice Girls and more surprises. :)

The Christmas set includes about 30-40 minutes of acoustic music (guitar & singing). Turning the audition you can hear the most known Christmas songs and also other beautiful songs which are creating a pleasant mood for your Christmas event. Jingle Bells, Last Christmas, Wonderful tonight, Dance with somebody and many others. Performance also includes equipment for the audition (EP speakers etc.).

Price 150€
Booking and additional information through Contact form or +358405182311
(suomi, english, estonia)

Aiki is a singer and song writer from Estonia. Aiki writes all her songs lyrics and melodies. She uses guitar to help make new songs. Aiki’s acoustic performances are sended by herself with guitar and some live gigs are performed also with a band whose members are Jori, Tuukka and Indro. Recordings are usually arranged together with band members.
